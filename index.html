Welcome to the

Wealthy Club

The WEALTHY PENGUIN ISLAND CLUB is an exclusive collection of 5 000 wealthy penguin NFTs—unique digital collectibles. They are stored as ERC-721 tokens on the Ethereum blockchain and hosted on IPFS. Your Wealthy Penguin also doubles as access to our private business network, the AIRPORT LOUNGE, a virtual networking lounge, and finally our NFT’s represent access to our future private island community accessible only to our members. More perks and benefits will be unlocked for our community throughout the evolution of our roadmap.

With more than 160+ hand drawn traits, each NFT is unique and represents a business card to our exclusive group of successful investors & philanthropists. Join our global community of diverse entrepreneurs with access to once in a lifetime experiences, benefits and utilities.

Features

An Exclusive Wealthy Club

In 2021 during the height of a global pandemic a call was made to global minded entrepreneurs in cities around the world to bring together the world's most successful entrepreneurs who want to give back. They were tasked with creating a new members club that would give back through philanthropy. This entity would eventually be called the Wealthy Penguin Club. A place where high level thinkers, investors, and entrepreneurs get together to network and solve the world's biggest problem while having fun. The final container of the project will be a new community on a private island only known to its members.

Philantrophic Focus

The Wealth Penguins care about giving back. We have set aside 10% of our primary sale to be donated to a charity voted on by our members. In addition, 5% of every secondary sale will be donated to vulnerable populations around the world forever. That’s truly giving back.

Lucky Penguins

7 of our penguins are lucky. They grant it’s owners passive income of .07 ETH per month for life. After our main sale we will airdrop the 7 penguins to lucky owners. Yes that’s right if you are lucky enough to get one of these then you will have .07 ETH sent to your account each month forever.

Inner Circle

PARTICIPATE IN ISLAND BUILDING

EVOLUTION OF THE RANKS

ANNUAL INCOME

CELEB ENTREPRENEURS
